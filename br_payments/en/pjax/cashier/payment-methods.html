How our payment works

How do I deposit funds into my ChampionFX account?

You can deposit funds into your ChampionFX account through our Cashier service by using credit/debit card or e-currency.

What currencies do you support for deposits?

We accept deposits in the following currencies: US Dollar, Great Britain Pound, Euro, and Australian Dollar.

Can I transfer funds between my accounts?

Yes, to fund your MT5 account(s), you must fund your ChampionFX real account first. Then, you will transfer funds from your ChampionFX real account into your MT5 account(s).

Can I deposit funds using a credit or debit card?

Absolutely. We accept deposits using the following cards: Visa, MasterCard, and China UnionPay.

Do you charge a fee for each deposit and withdrawal?

All deposit and withdrawal transactions are free of fees or commissions.

Is there a minimum deposit amount?

Minimum deposits for our Champion Trader platform range from USD 5 to USD 10, depending on the payment method you use. There are no minimum deposit requirements for our MT5 platform.

Do my deposit and withdrawal methods have to be similar?

Yes, if you’ve deposited funds into your ChampionFX account using a certain payment source, you will need to withdraw your funds using the same payment source.

How long does it take for deposited funds to appear in my account?

Deposits are processed by ChampionFX within 24 hours. However, your bank or payment service provider may require additional processing time.

How do I withdraw funds from my ChampionFX account?

You can withdraw funds from your ChampionFX account through our Cashier service by using the same payment method used to deposit funds into your account.

How long does it take for ChampionFX to process my withdrawal request?

Withdrawals are processed by ChampionFX within 24 hours. However, your bank or payment service provider may require additional processing time.

Can I see a history of all my deposits and withdrawals?

Yes, just log into ChampionFX and then select ‘Statement’ from the drop-down menu in the top right-hand corner of the window. You will be able to see all trades, deposits, and withdrawals that have taken place on your account.

Do you allow third-party payments?

Unfortunately, no. Under the terms of our financial license, third-party payments are not allowed due to its high risk for abuse.
