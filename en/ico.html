About ICO

What is an ICO?

An ‘Initial Coin Offering’ (or ‘ICO’ for short) is a fundraising method that’s relatively similar to an Initial Public Offering (IPO). In an ICO, cryptocurrency such as ‘coloured coins’ or ‘Ethereum tokens’ are issued instead of shares.

Why is ChampionFX performing an ICO?

ChampionFX is a new multi-asset broker launched in 2017. We believe that this ICO gives us a great opportunity to reach more people, in line with our mission to make financial trading more accessible to everyone. The funds raised from the ICO will also enable us to invest in the infrastructure and resources needed to penetrate and capture our target markets.

How will the ICO work?

ChampionFX will offer a number of blockchain tokens to investors. These tokens may be issued in various forms on different blockchains, such as ‘coloured coins’ on the Bitcoin blockchain, and ‘ERC20’ tokens on the Ethereum blockchain.

Regardless of the type of token, each token will provide you with two rights:

  1. The right to receive payments equivalent to dividends paid on Champion Group Ltd. Ordinary
  2. The right to convert tokens into Ordinary Shares

How to purchase ChampionFX token

How will the tokens be offered?

The tokens will be offered over a period of one month. The exact date of the ICO is yet to be determined, but we are aiming for the fourth quarter of 2017. The ICO price and auction method will be announced in due course.

How can I purchase your tokens?
